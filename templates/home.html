About Baymax

Baymax is inspired by Disney's Big Hero 6 — a compassionate, adorable AI healthcare companion. This project was built to bring Baymax's caring personality to life digitally, helping with health predictions, emergency guidance, and reading handwritten prescriptions.

About the creator: I'm Ragib Hasan, a student at Sylhet Engineering College, passionate about AI and healthcare technology. I built Baymax to make health tools friendly, accessible, and cute!
